Welcome to SpotViz!

What is This?

This site provides a historical playback of JT65/JT9 digital mode signals transmitted and received by amateur radio operators, showing patterns of signals over time.

How do I get started?

If you have already uploaded a data log file from WSJT, you can click on 'Visualize' above to pick a data/time range for playback of your data plotted on Google Maps. You can also view anyone else's data that has already been updated - click 'Browse Data' (TODO).

To upload new data, click 'How To Upload Your Spots'.
